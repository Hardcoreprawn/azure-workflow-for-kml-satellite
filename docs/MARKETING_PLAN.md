## 1. The Problem We Solve

### The real, distinct problem

**Thousands of organisations are legally required (EUDR) or mission-compelled
(conservation) to prove that specific land areas haven't been deforested — and
the only options are either free-but-complex dev tools (GEE, QGIS) or
enterprise-priced platforms (Planet at £2,700+/yr, GFW Pro with account request
gates, consultancies at £10K+ per engagement).**

There is nothing in the middle: a self-serve, no-code tool that takes a land
boundary and returns an auditable, AI-analysed evidence report for under £50/mo.

### Why this problem exists now

1. **EUDR enforcement is live.** Large operators since Dec 2025, SMEs from June
   2026. ~3,000+ large EU operators + tens of thousands of SMEs must produce
   geolocation-based deforestation-free evidence for every commodity shipment.
   Fines: 4% of EU-wide turnover.

2. **Free tools don't produce evidence.** GFW shows global dashboards — you
   can't export a per-site audit report. GEE requires JavaScript/Python and a
   commercial licence for paid work. QGIS requires a GIS degree and days of
   manual work.

3. **Commercial tools are gatekept.** Planet requires sales calls and £2,700+/yr.
   GFW Pro requires an account application (weeks). Consultancies charge
   £10K–50K per engagement.

4. **The gap is £0 → £2,700/yr.** Between "free but only for coders" and
   "enterprise sales call required," nobody serves the mid-market professional
   who just wants satellite evidence for their specific sites.

### Who has this problem

| Persona | Size of market | Urgency | Willingness to pay |
|---------|---------------|---------|-------------------|
| **EUDR compliance officers** at mid-sized EU importers (cocoa, coffee, palm oil, soy, wood, rubber, cattle) | ~3,000 large operators + tens of thousands of SMEs | **Critical** — regulatory deadline, 4% of turnover fines | Very high — compliance budgets |
| **ESG consultancies** doing EUDR audits for clients | ~500–2,000 firms in EU/UK | **High** — client demand surging | High — billable per engagement |
| **Conservation NGOs** monitoring protected areas | ~5,000–10,000 organisations globally | **Ongoing** — mission-critical | Medium — budget-constrained but desperate for efficiency |
| **Agricultural advisors / crop insurers** assessing land | Thousands of firms | **Seasonal** — event-driven | Medium — ROI-driven |

## 3. Competitive Landscape Summary

### Direct competitors (satellite + compliance/conservation)

| Competitor | What they do | Price | Weakness for our personas |
|-----------|-------------|-------|--------------------------|
| **Global Forest Watch (Free)** | Global deforestation dashboards, GLAD/RADD alerts | Free | No per-site custom analysis, no weather, no AI narrative, no exportable evidence reports |
| **GFW Pro** | Per-location analysis for enterprises, commodity risk | Free 5K MLAs/yr, then consumption-based ($20K+/yr tiers) | Account-request gated (not self-serve), deforestation only, enterprise-only pricing |
| **Sylva.earth** | Satellite + AI deforestation prediction, supply chain intel | Unknown (VC-backed) | Direct competitor — but requires demo/sales process, not self-serve |
| **Google Earth Engine** | 80+ PB geospatial data, any analysis via code | Free for research; commercial licence required for paid work | Requires JavaScript/Python proficiency; commercial licence for ESG/consultancy work |
| **Planet / Sentinel Hub** | High-res daily imagery (3.7m), now merged | £2,700–9,650/yr | Sells imagery, not analysis; requires GIS expertise to process |
| **Terrascope** | Carbon management SaaS (Scope 1–3) | Enterprise SaaS | Carbon accounting, not satellite evidence — different product category |
| **Trase** | Supply chain commodity flow mapping | Free (research/NGO) | Maps trade flows, not per-site satellite analysis |

### Indirect competitors

| Competitor | Overlap |
|-----------|---------|
| **QGIS + manual workflow** | The default — free but 50–100x slower |
| **Environmental consultancies** (ERM, WSP, Ramboll, boutiques) | Trusted but 1000x more expensive and weeks slower |
| **SkyFi** | Imagery marketplace ($25+/order) — sells raw imagery, not analysis |

### Where Canopex wins

1. **Self-serve.** No sales calls, no account applications. Sign up → upload → results.
2. **No code, no GIS.** KML upload is the entire interface.
3. **AI-analysed evidence.** Not raw data — a narrative explaining *what* changed and *why*.
4. **Weather + vegetation integrated.** "Was this cleared by humans or killed by drought?"
5. **Accessible price.** £19–149/mo vs. £2,700+/yr or £10K+ consultancy.
6. **No commercial licence restrictions.** Use for any purpose — NGO, commercial, government.

## 4. Business Model

### Revenue model: tiered SaaS subscriptions

| Tier | Monthly price | AOI limit | Key capabilities | Target persona |
|------|--------------|-----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Free** | £0 | 5 runs / 5 AOIs | Basic analysis, no AI, no export, 30-day retention | Tyre-kickers, students, researchers evaluating |
| **Starter** | £19 | 15 runs / 15 AOIs | AI insights, export (PDF/CSV/GeoJSON), 60-day retention | Individual conservation analysts, small NGOs |
| **Pro** | £49 | 50 runs / 50 AOIs | Everything in Starter + monthly temporal cadence, 90-day retention | Active conservation programmes, ESG consultants |
| **Team** | £149 | 200 runs / 200 AOIs | Everything in Pro + API access, 365-day retention, 10x concurrency | ESG consultancies, compliance teams, ag insurers |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Custom SLA, SSO/SAML, white-label, dedicated support | Large commodity traders, financial institutions |

### Why this model works

- **EUDR compliance officers** need 50–500 site assessments. Pro (£49, 50 AOIs)
  or Team (£149, 200 AOIs) covers most. Compare: consultancy at £200+/site.
- **Conservation NGOs** need 5–15 sites monitored monthly. Starter (£19, 15
  AOIs) is perfect and below their discretionary spend threshold (~£100/mo).
- **Overage pricing** (£1.50/AOI on Starter, £0.80 on Pro, £0.50 on Team) means
  burst usage doesn't require tier upgrade.
- **Free tier** is the conversion funnel — 5 runs lets them prove value before
  paying.
