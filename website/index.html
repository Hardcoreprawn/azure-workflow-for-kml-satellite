Questions

FAQ

Does this meet EUDR evidence requirements?
Canopex provides satellite-based supporting evidence for EUDR due diligence under Regulation 2023/1115. It produces timestamped, multi-source vegetation analysis with audit-grade reports. However, it does not constitute a complete due-diligence assessment — operators remain responsible for supply-chain traceability, in-country verification, and declaration submission.
What satellite data do you use?
Sentinel-2 L2A (10 m, global, 5-day revisit) for NDVI vegetation analysis, ESA WorldCover (10 m, 2020/21) for baseline land cover, NASA FIRMS for fire hotspots, and Open-Meteo for weather context. All data is accessed via Microsoft Planetary Computer. See our full methodology.
How many parcels can I assess?
Free trial includes 2 parcel assessments with no expiry. EUDR Pro includes 10 parcels/month with additional parcels at £3 each (volume discounts from 100+ parcels). Enterprise plans support 1,000+ parcels/month with annual commitment pricing.
What format is the report?
Each assessment produces a structured evidence package: an interactive dashboard with NDVI time series and map overlays, plus a downloadable PDF report with deforestation-free determination, confidence level, supporting evidence, data provenance, and methodology reference. GeoJSON and CSV exports are also available.
How long does an assessment take?
Most single-parcel assessments complete in under 5 minutes. Processing time depends on parcel size and cloud-cover history. Batch assessments run in parallel — 50 parcels typically complete within 15 minutes.
What is the EUDR cutoff date?
The EU Deforestation Regulation uses 31 December 2020 as the cutoff date. Canopex analyses vegetation from January 2021 onwards, comparing seasonal NDVI against this baseline to detect any post-2020 deforestation.
What happens to my data?
Uploaded coordinates and assessment results are stored securely in your organisation's isolated workspace. Data is retained for the duration of your subscription. We do not sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy.
