The Challenge

Satellite imagery has arrived. Most of it is wasted.

Manual Analysis is Expensive

Human interpreters spend hours analyzing satellite imagery per site. Cost scales linearly with area surveyed.

Did tree density change this season? Last year? You need a temporal database and the time to query it manually.

Data Silos Prevent Insights

KML files live in email. Imagery lives in cloud storage. Spreadsheets live in drives. No single source of truth.

How It Works

From KML to actionable insights in minutes.

1

Upload AOI

Define your area of interest as a KML file. Polygon, multipolygon, any shape.

2

Source Imagery

We query Planet Labs and USGS for cloud-free satellite imagery matching your AOI and date range.

3

Preprocess & Normalize

Geometric registration, atmospheric correction, NDVI calculation. All handled automatically.

4

Tree Detection & Classification

AI inference identifies trees, estimates health status, and flags anomalies.

5

Temporal Analysis

Compare across dates. Detect loss, regeneration, health trends, and seasonal patterns.

6

Export & Alert

GeoJSON, CSV, PDF, or JSON. Automatic alerts for significant changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Request early access using the form below. We'll send you a demo account within 24 hours. You can then upload KML files and see results immediately.

What imagery sources do you use?

Planet Labs (primary), USGS Landsat, and Sentinel-2. We prioritize cloud-free, high-resolution imagery for your AOI.

How long does analysis take?

Depends on AOI size and data availability. Typical: 5–30 minutes from upload to results. Large areas or multi-date requests may take longer.

What file formats do you support?

Input: KML, GeoJSON, Shapefile. Output: GeoJSON, CSV (tabular), PDF (reports), TIF (raster analysis).

Can I export results to ArcGIS or QGIS?

Yes. GeoJSON and Shapefile exports are compatible with any GIS platform. We can also integrate directly via REST API (in development).

What about privacy and data retention?

Your data is yours. We don't sell or share analysis results. By default, we retain processing data for 30 days, then delete. You can request immediate deletion.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. Beta users get free unlimited access. Pricing tiers (Free, Pro, Enterprise) launch when we exit beta.

Do you have an API?

In development. For now, we support web upload and direct file handling. REST API coming in Phase 5.
